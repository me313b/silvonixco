SILVONIX TECHNOLOGY

We develop high-performance power modules with integrated electronics for efficient, bidirectional energy conversion. Designed for automotive and aerospace, our modular, reliable, and long-lasting solutions support a sustainable energy future.

Team

Silvonix is at the forefront of innovation in power conversion and energy management, delivering cutting-edge solutions for electric vehicles and other demanding applications. Since its founding in 2025, the company has focused on redefining DC-to-AC conversion with ultra-compact, high-efficiency inverters. With breakthrough technology and a commitment to performance, Silvonix is driving advancements in one of the most dynamic and competitive industries worldwide.
